decision after S168's document-independent semantic NO-GO. It is a decision
review, not another mechanism iteration.

Both judges receive the same compact packet: current funnel, independent
metrics, transport counterfactual and the relevant prior synthesis mechanisms.
They assess five options. Exact convergence is required on whether to stop the
generic ledger line and on the recommended next option. Disagreement is HOLD;
there is no arbitration or second round.

The review has at most two calls, zero retries and a USD 1.50 internal ceiling.
It cannot authorize production, fact credit or a target probe. Its only possible
GO is permission to design and freeze a new local independent experiment.
