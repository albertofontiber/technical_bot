# S171 final bounded relation-store construction gate

S171 is the final transport-only successor to S170. It uses the different,
already sealed S147 development cohort and its deterministic 37-point
source-unit gold. No S168 question or label is reused.

S170 showed that valid rich chunks naturally produced 23–27 relations while
the local application silently expected at most 18; three other outputs reached
the 2,400-token boundary. S171 declares a maximum of 30 relations in both the
prompt and provider schema, allows 90 provenance assignments, requires concise
relation fields and raises the frozen extraction output limit to 5,000 tokens.
The relation ontology, one-call-per-chunk isolation, immutable provenance,
selector, scoring and 90/80/75 semantic gates do not change.

There are at most 28 Haiku calls, zero retries and a USD 2.00 ceiling. Any
construction or semantic NO-GO terminates the relation-store line. A pass still
requires a fresh document-independent promotion cohort and semantic-fidelity
audit before targets.
